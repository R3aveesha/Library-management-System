About Us

IABT University maintains two libraries at Colombo and Kandy. The library of the Clombo campus act as the main library. Both branches contain fields of Information Technology , Business Management, Computer Systems and Networking, Bio Technology and Food Science.IABT University provides access to their students a wide variety of resources, books, articles, research papers, CD, DVD, magazines journals, past papers and other subjects relaiting to the core teaching areas.
